Indraprasth Adhyayan Kendra

Study is a continuous process of life and there is also evidence of lively thinking. The Indraprastha Adhyayan Kendra is a platform in society in which intellectuals, academicians, administrators, research-students, professionals, entrepreneurs, workers, etc. of all classes and businesses sensitive to social concerns, consider the questions of their time and era.
